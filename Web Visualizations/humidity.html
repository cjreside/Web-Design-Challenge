Humidity

The humidity level does not appear to be strongly correlated with latitude based on this plot. There is some evidence that cities at the higher latitude are more humid. However, I don't believe we can come to a conclusion based on this plot, as the underlying data was just a snapshot of weather conditions based on a particular day and time. Further research would be needed.
